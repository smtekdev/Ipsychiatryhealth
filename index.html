Treatment What We Offer

Psycho Therapy

Psychotherapy is based on the premise that your thoughts, feelings, and actions are all interconnected, and that negative thoughts and feelings can imprison you in a vicious cycle.

Ketamine

Advanced medical care making life easier for those suffering from treatment-resistant/significant depression or who are on the verge of self-harm and want a quick and easy way to receive help.

Medication Management

Medication Management is an outpatient treatment that entails a professional’s initial assessment of the patient’s medication needs, issuing a prescription, and continuing medical monitoring of the patient’s medication use.

Psychological Testing

Psychological testing, also known as psychometrics, is the systematic use of tests to evaluate psycho-physical behavior, skills, and concerns, and to develop psychological performance forecasts.

Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation

Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) is a safe, drug-free, and insurance-approved treatment for depression provided by One Behavioral.
